🌍 7 Wonders of the World

🇮🇳 Taj Mahal

Located in India, the Taj Mahal was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

🇨🇳 Great Wall of China

One of the longest structures ever built, created to protect ancient China from invasions.

🇮🇹 Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome was used for gladiator battles and public spectacles during the Roman Empire.

🇲🇽 Chichen Itza

An ancient Mayan city in Mexico famous for its pyramid called El Castillo.

🇵🇪 Machu Picchu

A historic Incan city located high in the Andes Mountains of Peru.

🇯🇴 Petra

Petra is an ancient city in Jordan carved directly into pink sandstone cliffs.

🇧🇷 Christ the Redeemer

A giant statue of Jesus Christ overlooking Rio de Janeiro in Brazil.
